How it works

Simple. Smart. Actionable.

LactiQ is designed to collect meaningful physiological and behavioral signals, interpret them through farm-ready algorithms, and present clear next-step decisions in a practical dashboard.

1

Attach

Apply the smart patch at key monitoring areas such as behind the ear, near the heart area, or near the tail head and rear udder region.

2

Collect

Continuously capture temperature, motion, vibration, and blood flow related physiological signals.

3

Analyze

Use algorithms to identify signal combinations associated with calving, repro events, fever risk, and abnormal recovery patterns.

4

Act

Give herd teams alerts and cow-level priorities that support earlier checks, better timing, and stronger milk and health outcomes.
